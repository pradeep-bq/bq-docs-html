BQ Documentation

This site provides counts and fill rates for BQ data and products.

Product Family Product Description Counts Primary Use Case
Organization Firmographics This product provides the core firmographics of an Organization, including: primary company name; headquarters address (including lat-long); identifiers (EIN, CIK, LEI, state company numbers); TICKER (if public); complete set of industry classifications (NAICS, SIC, IRS, detailed categories); date founded; primary phone number; primary website; primary LinkedIn URL; legal form of organization; employment status (employer, non-employer, sole proprietor, holding company); for or non-profit status. As of Jan 2024, there are approximately 74 million Organizations, almost all of which have Firmographics. Business Identification & Entity Resolution; Sales & Marketing; Underwriting & Credit Risk; Screening; Compliance, KYC & Due Diligence; Financial Analysis & Benchmarking.
Organization Financials & Employment This product provides current and historical financials and employment of an Organization, including: monthly, quarterly, and annual employment and payroll; quarterly and annual income statement and balance sheet line items. Income statement line items include: revenue, gross margin, operating expenses, net income, EBITDA. Balance sheet line items include: total assets, cash, short and long term debt, accounts receivable and payable. BQ Credit Scores are also available. As of Jan 2024, there are approximately 74 million Organizations. Organizations with employees (“employers”) have quarterly and annual financials, and monthly employment and payroll. All other Organizations have annual financials. Employment for non-employers and sole proprietors is counted as the number of principals / officers. Underwriting & Credit Risk; Screening; Compliance, KYC & Due Diligence; Financial Analysis & Benchmarking.
Corporate Family Tree This product provides the corporate family tree of an Organization, including ultimate parent and children entities; the status of all member legal entities with their respective Secretary of State; compliance checks such as liens and OFAC watchlist; legal identifiers; and relationships to non-U.S. entities. This product provides the information at the Legal Entity level, which is then mapped to Organizations. As of Jan 2024, there are approximately 97 million legal entities, almost all of which have their corresponding corporate hierarchy. Compliance, KYC & Due Diligence; Underwriting & Credit Risk; Business Identification & Entity Resolution.
Legal Entity Directors & Officers This product provides the Directors and Officers of Legal Entities, including their official legal positions (ranked in order of importance); whether they are a person, company, or registered agent; and address (sometimes residential). A director or officer is an individual or company that exerts legal control over an entity. This product provides the information at the Legal Entity level, which is then mapped to Organizations. This product does not have emails or phones. As of Jan 2024, there are approximately 149 million Directors and Officers, all of which are mapped to Legal Entities. Compliance, KYC & Due Diligence; Underwriting & Credit Risk; Business Identification & Entity Resolution.
Organization Contacts This product provides all known Contacts associated with an Organization, including their job title and position; LinkedIn URL; phone (landline / mobile designation, direct / general); email (professional / personal); and address (business / residential). For SMBs, the address may be residential. As of Jan 2024, there are approximately 229 million Contacts, all of which are mapped to Organizations. Sales & Marketing.
Organization Locations This product provides all known addresses associated with an Organization. Addresses are based on legal addresses reported to Secretaries of State, official HQ addresses reported to regulatory agencies (IRS, SEC, SBA, etc.), office locations reported as having employees, and consumer-facing business locations (e.g., stores, restaurants). For SMBs, the legal and/or official address may be residential. All addresses are standardized using USPS methods. As of Jan 2024, there are approximately 288 million Locations, all of which are mapped to Organizations. Business Identification & Entity Resolution; Sales & Marketing.
Legal Entity Stock Filings This product provides the capital structure, filing history, and corporate events tied to all private companies with an ultimate parent in Delaware, which constitutes about 2/3 of large companies. From this product, one can infer changes that have taken plan in authorized shares, and their basic characteristics. As of Jan 2024, there are approximately 275 thousand legal entities actively being monitored in this fashion, all of which are mapped to Organizations having an ultimate parent in Delaware. Capital Markets; Compliance, KYC & Due Diligence; Underwriting & Credit Risk.
Organization Executives and Officers This product provides contact information for all Directors, Officers, and Executives associated with an Organization. It is created by combining the Contact information for all Directors & Officers associated with all Legal Entities that make up the Organization. This product includes their job title and position; LinkedIn URL; phone (landline / mobile designation, direct / general); email (professional / personal); and address (business / residential). This product includes VP-level employees, executives, and officers in general. As of Jan 2024, there are approximately 84 million Executives and Officers, all of which are mapped to Organizations. Sales & Marketing.
